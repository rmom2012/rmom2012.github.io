White Space / Clean Design

Web Design Yorkshire

webdesignyorkshire.com

The use of negative space for the Web Design Yorkshire website allows their work product to take center stage. Each image of a site that they have made stands out and invites the viewer to further explore thier website. Also, the clean design helps the user to quickly find what they are looking for without a lot of uneeded distractions.

Contrast

Sweet Hat Club

sweethatclub.org

The Sweet Hat Club web site effectively uses contrast to increase visual interest and organize thier website. The teal and yellow colors offer a crisp contrast to the sites background colors. Additionally, the contrast in font sizes helps the user better understand how the site is organzied.The Sweet Hat Club site is a great example of a fun website that uses simple color and font size to make their site stand out.

Repetition

Sleep Street

sleepstreet.be

The use of good repetition in the Sleep Street web site gives it a a very professional look and feel. A repeating color scheme in addition to an appealing grid pattern help the user to quickly find what they are looking for with confidence. Also, the consistent placement of logos and symbols keeps the site looking sleek and modern.
