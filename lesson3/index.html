Site Name

The name of the organizational site will be Simply Weather and the domain for the site will be called simplyweather.com.

Site Purpose

The focus of simplyweather.com will be to help educate users about the weather conditions in an specific location. In addition to providing weather predictions to specified locations, simplyweather.com will also issue severe weather warnings and educational opportunities for local schools. The site will provide daily forecasts that include predicted hourly weather forecasts. Additionally, extended 7-day forecasts will be provided so that the local population may be able to plan community activities. Weather-related alerts will also be posted to the site as well as the latest weather-related articles and a gallery of weather-related photos for use in community center education classes, and local schools
