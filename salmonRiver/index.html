Welcome to Salmon River Adventures

After 50 years guiding adventurous travelers through the most spectacular rivers and canyons in Idaho, Salmon River Adventures has carefully crafted what we consider to be the best white water rafting vacations in the world. On a Salmon River Expedition you will quickly discover why so many are calling this "the best vacation of their lives." Call 208-333-4444 today and let us help you find the perfect adventure.
